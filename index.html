Top 3 things to check out in Manitoba!

The Devils Punchbowl!

A rare and amazing underground spring in the middle of the only true Desert in Manitoba. Sand left over from ancient rivers and lakes creates a landscape with cactus you would only find in a Desert, surrounded by prairie features mixed in, a beautiful hike with ton's of opportunity to learn.

Winnipeg Jets!

Make your way to the peak of Hockey performance, the Winnipeg Jets are wildly entertaining, and have been incredibly competitive for the Stanley Cup in recent years! Grab a beer and cheer for the White Out!

Snow Machines!

Let's face it. Manitoba is COLD! For more than half of the year the activities available for fun are in the cold! Skiing, skating, sledding on a hill and the most fun of all, hoping on a Skidoo and blazing down a trail, hopefully you have heated handlebars!
